Our vision

A design system is a collection of pre-built, reusable assets—components, patterns, guidance, and code—that allows its users (UX Designers, Developers, PO's, PM's, etc…) to build consistent digital experiences faster. By using the pre-built and universal assets of Design System, the time teams spend designing and building is minimized. Instead of building and re-building basic elements, they can spend that time customizing their products to address specific client use cases. With Wellmark's Design System we want to provide a cohesive experience to our users while meeting the company's business needs.
