Typography

Font sizes are based on the Major Third Scale and rounded to the closest increment of four to match our spacing guidance. Major Third Scale is popular because it provides ample visual hierarchy while still accomodating lots of text content.

Developers are encouraged to use ems or rems instead of pixels.

Additional verbiage guidance is available in the Wellmark Brand Guide.
